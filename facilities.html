Academic & Science Laboratories

Hands-on learning through experimentation and practical application

Physics Lab

The Physics laboratory is furnished with a diverse range of equipment that enhances and reinforces the concepts taught in the classroom. Students are carefully guided through experiments, with attention paid to every fine detail. This thorough approach ensures that the knowledge gained is deeply understood and retained in their minds for the future.

Chemistry Lab

Our Chemistry Laboratory meets the standards of a well-equipped, high-quality lab. It provides students with the opportunity to transform theoretical knowledge into practical application. The lab is fully stocked with the necessary instruments and chemicals for conducting experiments such as salt analysis and volumetric analysis. A supportive and student-friendly environment encourages active learning, helping to build confidence and enhance the overall development of students through continuous guidance and reinforcement.

Biology Lab

Our Biology laboratory is spacious, well-ventilated, and equipped with modern facilities to meet current academic requirements. Designed in accordance with the latest syllabus, it houses a rich collection of models, specimens, charts, and essential materials. The lab emphasizes practical learning alongside theory, ensuring a balanced approach that supports the overall development of students.

Computer Lab

Well-equipped computer labs provide students with access to the latest developments in Information Technology. Apex International Senior Secondary School features a dynamic computing environment with high-end systems, modern technology, and advanced software. Both junior and senior students gain hands-on experience and a strong foundation in computer skills with supportive teachers.

Important Guidelines

  • 1 Parents who wish to avail the school bus facility for their children must submit an application to the Principal in advance.
  • 2 Discipline must be maintained on the bus at all times. Actions such as shouting, making noise, throwing objects, or passing remarks at people outside are strictly prohibited. Strict action will be taken against those who break these rules.
  • 3 Any request for a change in boarding point or discontinuation of the transport service must be submitted in writing at least one month in advance.
  • 4 In case the school bus is unable to operate on any day due to unforeseen circumstances, students are expected to arrange their own transport.
